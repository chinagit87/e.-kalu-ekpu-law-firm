Nigeria Social Insurance Trust Fund Certificate (NSITF) verifies that a company is registered and contributing to
the social insurance scheme for employees who suffer work-related injuries, diseases, disabilities, or death.

This certificate is often mandatory for securing government contracts and demonstrates a company's commitment to its legal
obligations and employee welfare.

Why NSITF Certificate is important:
  • Contract eligibility: Companies without an NSITF certificate cannot register on the National database of
    contractors and are often ineligible for public contracts.
  • Credibility: It enhances a company's reputation as a reliable and responsible employer.
  • Legal compliance: Holding the certificate shows a company is meeting its legal obligations regarding
    employee compensation.
