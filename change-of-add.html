To change your address, you need to update your details with various institutions like your bank, employer, and utility providers,
and inform friends and family. It's also advisable to set up a mail redirection to ensure you don't miss important post during the
transition.

What happens if I don't do a change of address?

Failing to update your address promptly can lead to missed mail, delayed benefits, legal complications, and even identity theft risks.

Can my permanent address be different from where I live?

Unlike temporary or mailing addresses, your permanent address acts as your home base; it's your home of record, even if you spend
time living elsewhere. But what makes an address truly “permanent”? It's the place where you reside, and it's typically listed on crucial
documents such as your driver's license or passport.
